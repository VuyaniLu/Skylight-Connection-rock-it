How Skylight Works

Skylight Connection is an advanced AI-driven bot that can make up to 1000 phone calls every day. All you need to do is upload a CSV file with your clients’ phone numbers and names, and the bot will handle the rest! Our AI bot sounds so human-like, it’s almost impossible to tell it apart from a real person.

The bot works with real-time data to ensure every conversation is relevant. It can handle customer inquiries, appointment reminders, and much more – all customized to the industry type you provide.
